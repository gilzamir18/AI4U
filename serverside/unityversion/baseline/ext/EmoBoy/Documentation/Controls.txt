Movements:
	- Run:		WASD or Arrows
	- Walk:		Hold Left-Shift
	- Jump:		Space
	- Crouch:	Hold Left-CTRL/CMD

You can change these inputs in the "Settings/Inputs/Input Actions" asset.

---

Facial expressions:
	- Neutral:		Numpad 0
	- Happy:		Numpad 1
	- Sad:			Numpad 2
	- Angry:		Numpad 3
	- Surprised:	Numpad 4
	- Speak "A":	Numpad 5
	- Speak "E":	Numpad 6
	- Speak "O":	Numpad 7
	- Speak "F":	Numpad 8
	- Speak "L":	Numpad 9

You can change these inputs in the InputController.cs script.
